Fitness

I was blessed with a healthy and strong body (I have my father to thank for that). At around the age of 14 I started doing 200 pushups and 50 chin-ups a day. The results were incredible, and I really enjoyed working out! Though many see it as a difficult endeavor and a painful one at that, I view physical fitness as an essential road to self-love and discipline.

" In this world, the only thing we truly own is the body we shape every day " - Sun Ken Rock Manga

I started going to the gym semi-regularly. While my physique did improve, I could tell it wasn't for me. Lifting weights was uncomfortable, janky and didn't seem to target my muscles the same way calisthenics did. But in the spirit of self-improvement and getting better at things, I'd definitely say that going to the gym is something I intend to do much more this summer!
